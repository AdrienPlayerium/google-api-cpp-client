Contribute

This library is an open source project GitHub.

If you are interested in submitting fixes or enhacements, please contact us through the Google APIs Client Library for C++ Google Group for the time being. You may also email the group at google-api-cpp-client@googlegroups.com

A more formal process will be put in place in a future release.
